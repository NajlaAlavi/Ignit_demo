CONTEMPORARY SYLLABUS

The ignIT faculty includes professionals working in the field of application development. This helps in bringing real life situations into the curriculum.

By the end of the course, students are reqired to develope and publish a running application on the Google Play Store with guidance from the mentors. The app goes into the students portfolio.

Students get to work on actual commissioned projects. This gives them the chance to experience actual case scenarios and not just textbook examples.

Our syllabus is modified regularly according to changing trends in technology. Thus students are always in sync.
